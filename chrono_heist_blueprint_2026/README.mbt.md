# Chrono Heist Blueprint 2026

A top-down stealth puzzle built around short time loops.
Every loop records your route. On the next loop, that path replays as a ghost,
so the solution is about coordinating with your past selves.

## Controls

- `WASD` or arrow keys: move one tile
- `Space`: wait for one turn
- `R`: restart the current level
- `Enter`: start from title / return to title after full clear

## Objective

1. Reach the key.
2. Reach the vault with the key to copy the blueprint.
3. Escape through the extraction tile.

Guards patrol and can spot the current player or ghosts in straight lines.
Ghosts can stand on pressure plates and open doors, which is the core puzzle mechanic.

## Levels

- Level 1: learn ghost plate timing
- Level 2: chain key, vault, and extraction gates
- Level 3: use multiple loops to misdirect guards and open the final route

## Notes

The game is intentionally deterministic and turn-based so loop planning stays readable.
