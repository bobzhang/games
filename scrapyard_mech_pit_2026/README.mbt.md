# Scrapyard Mech Pit 2026

An arena survival game where your mech build evolves between waves through
salvaged parts. Weapon, chassis, and booster choices change how the run feels,
and plating or overclock upgrades push late-wave scaling.

## Controls

- `Enter` or left click on title: start
- `WASD`: move
- Mouse: aim
- Hold left mouse: fire
- `Space`: dash in your current movement direction
- `1`, `2`, `3` or click a card: pick salvage between waves
- `R`: restart after defeat or full clear

## Objective

Survive six waves of scrap pit enemies, then finish the final salvage choice to
clear the run.

## Build Notes

- `Rivet Cannon`: slower, heavier hits
- `Scatter Saw`: close-range spread pressure
- `Arc Welder`: high fire rate, lighter rounds
- `Bastion Frame`: high health, lower speed
- `Skirmisher Rig`: low health, high mobility
- `Magnet Coil`: stronger scrap pickup radius for sustain and economy
